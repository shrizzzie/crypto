Essential Tips to Avoid Crypto Scams

  • Never share your private keys or wallet seed phrases with anyone
  • Be skeptical of guaranteed returns or "too good to be true" investment opportunities
  • Verify the authenticity of websites and social media accounts
  • Research thoroughly before investing in any cryptocurrency project
